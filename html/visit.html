Accessibility

The museum has wheelchair accessibility ramps. It also has audio guides and braille display signs for the visually impaired. Feel free to contact the front desk if you have any questions or concerns. Inclusivity is very important at CSM, and we hope that all members of our community will be welcome and comfortable here!

Museum Café

There is a café attached to the museum where you can get light lunches, soft drinks, coffee, snacks and more. The menu will change according to the season, with updates posted regularly on our social media accounts.

Common allergens will be clearly listed on all menu items, but please feel free to ask if you have any concerns regarding allergies or other dietary restrictions. Full ingredient lists are available upon request.

Field Trips

Inspire the next generation of scientists!

Participating in school field trips to a local science museum provides students with a captivating and enriching learning experience. These excursions offer a unique opportunity for students to engage with interactive exhibits, hands-on activities, and engaging demonstrations that foster a deeper understanding of scientific concepts. Additionally, interacting with museum staff and experts can inspire students to pursue STEM-related interests and careers.
